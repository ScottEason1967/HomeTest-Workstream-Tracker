The written visit summary identifies home-testing value in the second group-and-save sample, MRSA clearance follow-up swabs for out-of-area patients (St James's takes tertiary referrals from as far as Devon and Northern Ireland), the pre-urology urine dip and baseline bloods at referral. It widens the acute pipeline beyond RDUH but sits at discovery, with no MoU or commercial step yet.
